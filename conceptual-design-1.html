Conceptual Design - Concept 1

Domain-Agnostic Solutions

Solution Description

There are two flavors of this solution, a technological and non-technological solution, but with a common root.

A user-definable checklist is the essence of this solution.

Considerations for non-technological solution

Pros
  • No technology required - widest possible market
  • Physical reminder as additional supportive trigger to action
  • Easy for the user to add and remove habits from list
  • Simple model to comprehend
Cons
  • Relies heavily on willpower
  • There is no assisted progression or adaptivity except for any initiative the user takes.
  • There is no encouragement other than the satisfaction of a completed checklist
  • There are no metrics or tracking available
  • Potential habits limited to slots defined on board
  • Environmental impact of raw materials and production process to produce a chalkboard or whiteboard

Additional Capabilities of a Technological Solution

The analogous technological solution could be implemented as a mobile phone application that provided the same logical capability, but with providing some additional features that remove several of the negatives associated with the non-technical solution.

The electronic version could:
  • allow a user to manage(create update and delete in this case) a list of desired habits (in this case freed of any list size restrictions)
  • provide a mechanism to see and check off the tasks daily
  • reset the completion statuses daily at the end of the day
  • provide the facility on completion of the list to trigger the display of motivational quotes, other helpful tips on habit formation, healthy eating, stress reduction, etc, and display positive trends in user's completion statuses.
  • Optionally the user could upload a picture of a loved one
  • If uploaded, the picture could be displayed when the app is started as an additional motivation to perform the actions

This pair of solutions lend themselves to a person who has a clear set of goals in mind and has the knowledge and will power to execute the habit formation process.
