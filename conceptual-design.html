Conceptual Design Exploration

Key Concepts

  • All designs are based on the concept of chaining of triggers for habit formation, linking the completion of one habit to trigger the execution of the next habit in a chain
  • The initial trigger is the user waking. The idea is for the user to execute one of these concepts immediate on rising, before doing anything else.

Discussion

Concept 3 appears to be the most promising when considering the design criteria. For users looking for a more supported experience, this design concept provides the richest opportunity to inject a flexible, evolving habit pattern, with the capacity to adapt to user's actual usage and needs. This also allows a platform to apply research findings to domain specific contexts. This concept will be further developed into mockups and prototypes.

The other solutions provide value and have favorable characteristics like streamlined designs, flexibility, and low barriers to adoption, but this project will next focus its resources initially on moving forward with concept 3. The project will keep the door open for other solutions or to revisit these concepts in the future, particularly given the iterative nature of design. The project encourages other project teams interested to further develop any of these concepts further.
