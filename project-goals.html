Guiding Principles

  • to provide a tool or tools that support the user in building beneficial habits
  • to seek to be an agent for good - respect users' privacy, empathize with their challenges, and cheer their victories
  • to provide transparency into the inner workings of the project as the project progresses - deliverables will hold appropriate open souce licenses
