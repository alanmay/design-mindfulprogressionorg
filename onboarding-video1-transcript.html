Welcome to MindfulProgress! The pace of modern life is staggeringly fast. Making time for what you find important is difficult. With so many interruptions in the middle of the day, it is challenging to consistently carve out time for yourself.

What if you started your morning out with some positive actions rather than reading your email, checking facebook, repeatedly hitting snooze?

You... you are the architect of that morning ritual, and we'll be here to support you. You select the behaviors you want in your morning ritual, and we will slowly build these behaviors up.

Building positive behaviors into habits using motivation alone is tough, REALLY TOUGH. Have you experienced this? I know I've had hard days and all of the sudden I've had a setback on my progress.

The key is to work on making the process of doing the right things easier, even if we have to take some baby steps to get the process going.

To get us started, we'd like you to do one 20 second exercise. Smile. Smile for 20 seconds with not a "I've got to smile at my dentist before he drills four caviities" smile, but a hello old friend kind of smile. You may want to check in the mirror, you will see skin folding around your eyes in a genuninely felt kind of smile.

So imagine an old friend you haven't seen in a long time, and send him some good thoughts for twenty seconds! Let that smile be free.
