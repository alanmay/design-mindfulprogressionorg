Conceptual Design - Concept 4

Domain-Aware Single Time of Day Targeted Solution

Solution Description

This would be a solution that focuses in on one specific need. Following the data, the biggest requests were for fitness and nutrition supports. So this might entail building a fitness application, in the vein of Sworkit or 7 MWC that is intended to support a morning workout. There is significant exploration needed to seek out what to bring into existence that adds to the breadth of choices for consumers.

One option for such a solution could be a fitness tool that targets the least fit individuals, providing them with supports for very gradually increasing their fitness through a conservative carefully planned morning workout. In this case, careful consideration of handling of user setbacks should be a focus.

Pros
  • Opportunity to build a single use application - ability to apply narrow focus and craft a polished solution to a high impact problem
Cons
  • Only addresses one issue
  • Harder to anchor to an existing behavior (unless the user has such a behavior that they want to use, such as waking.)
