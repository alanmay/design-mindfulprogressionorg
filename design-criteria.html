Design Criteria

Based on domain specific user research, the solution should:

  • focus on positive habit building (from original design inquiry)
  • be an early morning activity (identified in needs finding survey #1)
  • be time efficient - hover at or below 43 minutes to complete. Being short will also try to not exacerbate lack of sleep conditions most survey respondents reported (identified in Needs Finding Survey #1)
  • hit as many of the top categories of positive habits as possible (identified in needs finding survey #1)
  • motivate the user, encourage consistency
  • tap into top motivations (identified in needs finding survey #2)

Based on other key design needs, the solution should:

  • leverage insights from the psychology of behavior, particularly the Fogg Behavior Model
  • attempt to adhere to Jakob's Nielsen's Usability Heuristics
