Conceptual Design - Concept 5

Domain-Aware Repeated Small Action Targeted Solution

Solution Description

This solution is a variation of concept 4, but is intended to greatly accelerate habituation, by making the task small, but make the triggers frequent. The task itself will need to be something pleasurable in and of itself for a solution like this to be effective. An example of such a behavior might be to relax the muscles in ones back through a stretch or to take a deep breath.

Pros
  • A behavior that the user performs multiple times a day is likely to become a habit much more quickly and become deeper engrained. An hourly trigger during traditional working hours might be a reasonable approach.
Cons
  • Only addresses one issue
